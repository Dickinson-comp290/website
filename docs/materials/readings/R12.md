# Reading 12

Read _Chapter 12: Monitoring_ in [Cooperative Software Development](https://faculty.washington.edu/ajko/books/cooperative-software-development/) by Amy J. Ko.

Discussion Questions:

1. What is monitoring and how is it similar to and different from verification?
2. What techniques are used for monitoring and what makes it difficult?
3. Are there any ethical issues associated with monitoring?
